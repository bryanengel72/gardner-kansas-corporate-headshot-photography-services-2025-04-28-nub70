Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my professional headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that matches your industry standards. For corporate roles, traditional business attire works best. Creative professionals can opt for business casual. We'll provide a detailed styling guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Standard sessions run 45-60 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses, expressions, and outfit changes. We ensure you're comfortable and confident throughout the process.

What's included in the final package?

You'll receive 3-5 fully retouched, high-resolution digital images with multiple crops for different platforms. Additional images and formats are available for purchase.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Your professionally edited photos will be delivered within 3-5 business days via a secure digital gallery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer group corporate sessions?

Yes, we specialize in corporate team photography. We offer special rates and convenient scheduling for teams of 5 or more, ensuring consistent styling and branding across all images.
